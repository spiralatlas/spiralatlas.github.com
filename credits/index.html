Credits and Acknowledgements

These are assets used across my 3D Blender projects and visual novels. Most are creative commons, but a few were commercial purchases. Not all actually require credit to be given but this way the file can also stand as a recommendation of useful assets. Some assets have been added to this list that I didn’t end up actually using.

Acknowledgement of Country

I acknowledge the Whadjuk people of Noongar Boodjar as the Traditional Custodians of the Country in which this game was created. I recognise their continued connection to the land and waters of this beautiful place, and acknowledge that they never ceded sovereignty. I respect all Whadjuk Elders and Ancestors, and any First Nations people reading.
